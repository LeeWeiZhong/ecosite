Why use our website?

- Raise awareness on sustainable online brands. We want to let our users know which brands use eco-friendly packaging and which brands do not so that consumers will hopefully shop at those places with non-eco-friendly packaging less or just not buy from there altogether. At the same time, also encouraging them to buy from online businesses that use sustainable packaging and to show them that there are better sustainable choices in online shopping brands.

- Educate users on what makes an online business sustainable. Recently, many people have been interested in starting their own big or small business. We want to let them know what type of packaging they can use that is sustainable and eco-friendly so that they will not use those that are unsustainable and cannot be recycled like non-recyclable paper and plastic.

We aim to aid the movement for sustainability and hope to help others to play a part too in becoming green and clean by buying from sustainable online brands that use eco-friendly packaging.
